About Me

Student. Artist. Coder.

Hey, I'm Shourya — an aspiring design student about to start my college journey. I've spent the last couple of years teaching myself 3D modelling, digital illustration, and programming because I genuinely love creating things that didn't exist before.

I'm drawn to the space where design meets technology — where a well-crafted model, a clean script, or a striking illustration can communicate something words can't. I'm still early in my journey, and that's exactly what excites me.

I'm looking to study design formally and grow from a self-taught creator into someone who can shape how people experience the digital world. Big goals, small beginnings — and that's okay.
